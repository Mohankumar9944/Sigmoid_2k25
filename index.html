About US

Sigmoid is a annual technical festival of the Sri Venkateswara University College of Engineering(SVUCE) conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering(ECE) in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. It's a two-day event that features innovation, talent encouraging, entertainment, fun and exciting activities.

Technical Events

Technical event is designed to provide a platform for students to explore, innovate, and showcase their technical expertise through various competitions, and interactive sessions.

NON-TECH
Events

Non-Technical events focus on creativity, leadership, communication, and entertainment, providing students with opportunities for personal growth, teamwork, and enjoyment.

Workshops

Workshops are interactive training sessions designed to enhance practical knowledge and hands-on skills in a specific area. They can be industry-focused, helping students and professionals gain expertise beyond theoretical and practical learning.
